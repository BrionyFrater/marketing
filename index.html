About SignSync

The AI solution connecting JSL and spoken language

SignSync™ leverages artificial intelligence to translate Jamaican Sign Language (JSL) into English. We designed this technology to bridge communication barriers between deaf and hearing individuals and raise awareness about JSL.

The AI behind SignSync was trained in consultation with the Jamaica Association for the Deaf with the goal of accurately interpreting JSL.

Real Time Translation

Capture live sign language gestures and immediately display English translations.

JSL to Text

Uses AI model to instantly translate Jamaican Sign Language gestures into text with accuracy and real-time responsiveness.

Text to JSL

Effortlessly convert text input into Jamaican Sign Language videos, sourced from our comprehensive database.

How does it Work

SignSync uses artificial intelligence to accurately interpret Jamaican Sign Language (JSL) from video data. When you upload a video or sign live through the camera, the video information is processed by our AI model. The AI was trained using data from various individuals performing different signs, enabling it to recognize a variety of sign language gestures. Once the AI finishes processing a video, it instantly displays the English translation as text. Additionally, SignSync facilitates Text to JSL translation by enabling users to search our comprehensive database using English phrases to find corresponding JSL videos.

NB The Real-Time feature is not currently integrated into the website's frontend.
