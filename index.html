About SignSync

The AI solution connecting JSL and spoken language

SignSync© leverages artificial intelligence to translate Jamaican Sign Language (JSL) into English. We designed this technology to bridge communication barriers between deaf and hearing individuals and raise awareness about JSL.

The AI behind SignSync was trained in consultation with the Jamaica Association for the Deaf with the goal of accurately interpreting JSL.

How does it Work

SignSync uses artificial intelligence to accurately interpret Jamaican Sign Language (JSL) from video data. When you upload a video or sign live through the camera, the video information is processed by our AI model.

The AI was trained using data from various individuals performing different signs, enabling it to recognize a variety of sign language gestures. Once the AI finishes processing a video, it instantly displays the English translation as text. Additionally, SignSync facilitates Text to JSL translation by enabling users to search our comprehensive database using English phrases to find corresponding JSL videos.

Why use SignSync?

While sign language translation technology is available for other sign language variations, there is currently no publicly accessible resource specifically for JSL translation. This lack of resources exacerbates communication difficulties, especially considering the time and complexity required to learn JSL, which may be challenging for the majority of Jamaicans. SignSync breaks down the language barrier, allowing you to start communicating immediately with the deaf people you might encounter in day to day life.
